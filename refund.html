Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

Thank you for shopping with Sunamma’s Kerala Foods & Spices. We value your trust in our handmade, preservative-free products. Please read this Refund and Cancellation Policy carefully before placing your order.

1. Nature of Our Products

All our products are handmade in small batches using traditional methods. Due to their perishable and food-grade nature, we are unable to accept returns once a product has been delivered.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled within 6 hours of placing them by writing to us at talktosunamma@gmail.com.
  • Cancellations will not be accepted once the product has been shipped or prepared.

3. Refund Policy

Refunds will be considered under the following conditions:

  • You received a damaged or spoiled product.
  • You received an incorrect product.

To initiate a refund request:

  1. Email us at talktosunamma@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery.
  2. Include your order ID and clear photos of the product and packaging.

Once reviewed and approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 7 working days.

4. No Refunds in the Following Cases

  • If the product is disliked due to taste or spice level.
  • If you provide an incorrect shipping address.
  • If you fail to receive the delivery or delay pickup from courier.
  • If the issue is reported beyond 48 hours from delivery.

5. Contact Information

For any questions or to initiate a cancellation or refund:

Email: talktosunamma@gmail.com
Subject Line: "Refund Request - Order [Your Order ID]"

We are committed to delivering quality, and we’ll do our best to resolve any issues quickly and fairly.

Thank you for supporting handmade, homegrown food from Kerala!
